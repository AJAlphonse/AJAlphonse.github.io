The Story

Originating from the Blue Hills of Virginia, Guy Esguerra has always had a profound respect and appreciation for the rugged simplicity of life. It is no wonder he left his job as an air traffic controller to pursue his dream of woodworking.

Now Guy spends most of his time, sourcing and working with the finest quality goods, to bring you the most beautiful wood-crafted objects imaginable.
